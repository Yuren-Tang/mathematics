1. The pair complex is the compatibility centre; tensor and code theories are downstream reductions.

4. The complete fixed-lift target object is the full dual triangulation $T_g$, not merely the old-colour quotient $J_g$.
5. Gauge/Petrial motion and horizontal Fano-flow motion are distinct levels.
6. Finite obstruction cores occur on affine subspaces, but arrangement coverage and source composition remain separate problems.

9. BBD per-loop alternatives are controlled by one canonical defect flow; DDD remains a one-bit cohomological exception.
10. The sharp frontier is full-rank atom localisation and composition, not further undirected census.

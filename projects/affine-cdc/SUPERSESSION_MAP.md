## 2. OR1 fixed-lift versus fixed-fibre correction

> Affine compatibility automatically gives an orientable cycle-face surface for every compatible lift.

- one prescribed lift has obstruction
  $$
  \omega(g)\in C^1(G)/\operatorname{Cut}(G);

  $$
  \Omega_f\in C^1(G)/(\operatorname{Cut}(G)+\mathcal B_f).

## 2. Continuing mode

The v6 resolved-call graph and rank

\[
d_N(x)=\operatorname{dist}_{M_N}(x,\mathcal X_N)
\]

control continuing mode.

A continuing call macro consists of:

1. selected parent cancellation;
2. lower witnessed execution beginning at the inherited child flow;
3. source-faithful returned history;
4. labelled bubble compression;

Every chosen continuing macro lowers `d_N`. A child terminal does not form a continuing macro edge; it switches irreversibly to terminal mode.

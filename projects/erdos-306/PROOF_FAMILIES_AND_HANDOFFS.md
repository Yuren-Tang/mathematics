# Proof families and handoffs

## 1. Preferred proof family

The preferred mathematical exposition is:

```text
PNT
-> tail-uniform Abel local reciprocal law
-> common-threshold analytic interface
-> finite Fourier/control construction
-> arithmetic closure.
```

Reasons:

- it isolates the smallest construction-facing analytic interface;
- one local asymptotic supplies both density and reciprocal mass;
- it avoids hard-wiring release-specific explicit constants into the conceptual proof;
- it makes parameter eventuality and common thresholds transparent.

This family is `HUMAN-PROVED`, not released Lean authority.

## 2. Released formal proof family

```text

-> same finite Fourier/control construction

```

This family is `LEAN-CLOSED` relative to the two named assumptions.

It is not superseded as authority by the preferred PNT exposition. It remains the controlling machine-checked theorem family.

## 5. Local proof families

### Control-load proof

An elementary dyadic primorial/cardinality route proves $S_k=O(1/k)$. It is independent of PNT and RS after block density has supplied the lower deviation estimate.

### Low-energy forcing proof

Covering plus reciprocal dispersion forces either one dominant class or a quantitative energy floor.

### Global-control proof

A four-layer finite encoder and Laplace conversion give the level-set and partition bounds.

### Major-arc proof

Principal logarithm plus exact mean cancellation gives a Gaussian lower bound.

### Sibling proof

Squarefree CRT mismatch plus a common prime reservoir gives multiplicative damping.

Each is preserved as a distinct reusable mechanism.
